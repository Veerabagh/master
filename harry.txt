BIGQUERY STANDARD VS ENTERPRISE VS ENTERPRISE PLUS
Standard Edition
•	Basic tier: Suitable for testing, development, and small-scale workloads.
•	Limited features: Offers core BigQuery functionalities.
•	Cost-effective: Best for those starting out with BigQuery or with smaller datasets.
Enterprise Edition
•	Mid-tier: For businesses with growing data needs and requiring more advanced features.
•	Enhanced features: Includes improved security, governance, machine learning, and data management tools.
•	Balanced price: Offers a good balance between cost and features.
